About Me

Hi, I'm Toyang from the Philippines! I'm the woman behind UinsfireMe and Words by BTS. I'm a solid BTS' A.R.M.Y. (2017-05-01) but sometimes stan Ateez, Z Boys, SB19, Jackson Wang, SaltnPaper and The Rose. I am a fan of Asian dramas, movies and web series, mostly Korean, Chinese and Thai, and their foods and cultures.

I love actors and actresses like Xu Kai (许凯), Shin Ye-en, Park Seo-joon, Park Bo-gum, Song Hye-kyo, Han Hyo-joo, and many more.

I also love some Western stuffs like X-Men, Loving Caliber, Angelina Jolie, Ne-Yo, Chris Brown, Bruno Mars, Jason Mraz, Adele, Super Man, Noah Centineo, Can Yaman, etc. but it's not for this blog.

I can speak Tagalog (native), English (moderate), and some Bisaya and Korean (beginner). I can only read and write in Latin, Baybayin and Korean.

When I have time, I edit some photos of BTS with a quotation from them. I posted them on Words by BTS (방탄소년단의 말) Tumblr and Twitter account @wordsbybangtan.
